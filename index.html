Tamarindo

Regarding its nutritional contribution, we can say that it is a highly nutritious fruit, perhaps one of the non-oil source fruits that contains less water (31.4%). Its pulp is a good source of energy thanks to its carbohydrates. One hundred grams of tamarind pulp represents 239 calories.
