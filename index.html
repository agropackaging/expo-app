Very good! Jackfruit is considered a super fruit for all its health benefits. Jackfruit contains an impressive amount of essential nutrients such as fiber, protein, potassium, magnesium, folate, zinc, and vitamins A, C, E, and B.

Tamarindo

Regarding its nutritional contribution, we can say that it is a highly nutritious fruit, perhaps one of the non-oil source fruits that contains less water (31.4%). Its pulp is a good source of energy thanks to its carbohydrates. One hundred grams of tamarind pulp represents 239 calories.

Mango

The mango fruit is rich in (palmitic, myristic, malic and p-coumaric acid), vitamin C and, especially, due to its high content of vitamin A, the mango fruit constitutes a good antioxidant fruit.
